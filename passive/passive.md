## Power dissipation in passive components <a name="power"></a>
>- Power dissipation
>   - $P = I \times V$

## Resistors <a name="resistors"></a>
>- resistance
>   - $R = \frac{V}{I}$
>- conductance
>   - $G = \frac{1}{R}$
>- power
>   - $P_R = I^2R = \frac{U^2}{R}$

## Capacitors <a name="capacitors"></a>
>- voltage & current
>   - $u(t)=\frac{1}{C}\int{i(t)dt}$
>   - $i(t)=C\frac{dv(t)}{dt}$
>- stored charge
>   - $U_C = \frac{1}{2}CV^2$

## Inductors <a name="inductors"></a>
>- voltage & current
>   - $u(t)=L\frac{di}{dt}$
>   - $i(t)=\frac{1}{L}\int{u(t)dt}$
>- stored charge
>   - $U_L = \frac{1}{2}LI^2$
